Groups at our hostel

Accomodation

According to budget and gender, for groups we offer multi-bed rooms (4-6 person) with en-suit shower/WC or with shared showers in the hallway.

Morning Board

Our delicious all-you-can-eat breakfast buffet (07:00-11:00 a.m.) offers you a selection of bread rolls, sliced meats, cheese, and jam as well as tea and coffee.

Safety

Your safety is important to us! That’s why our reception is permanently manned (24/7), we use a secure key card system for room and locker access, we serve alcoholic beverages only to adults and the consumption of alcoholic beverages in the rooms is not permitted.

Extras

On-site bicycle rental, city tours etc. (reservations required).
