Welcome

Welcome to the Youth Hostel in the heart of London. No matter if you come to explore the city, plus beautiful area
around it, or just stop over flying in or out via London biggest Airport, we are happy to be your home away from
home. Clean, new facilities with awesome staff.

Our House - an old factory building from the turn of the century - was restored and developed in
1995. The typical Berlin-Kreuzberg charme of a brick building has been retained with light, spacious
rooms and a courtyard garden. On each floor you can find large bathrooms.
